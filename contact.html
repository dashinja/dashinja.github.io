What this site does

This is Byron Miller's portfolio home base. It introduces the developer, shows past work, shares contact details, and now points directly to Auntie Kim Dates.

How to use it

  1. Use About Me to read the bio and background.
  2. Use Portfolio to browse projects and open live demos.
  3. Select Auntie Kim Dates to jump to the family care app.
  4. Use Theme to switch between the two dark looks.
  5. Use Help anytime you need a quick refresher.
